The Best Movies According to Rahul

My top 3 movies of all-time.

KGF - Kolar Gold Fields

The story revolves around a poor child who transforms into the most prominent businessman and criminal, driven by a promise made to his ailing single mother on her deathbed.

Salaar

It's a dark tale of two best friends whose tribes are sworn enemies of each other.

Vikram

The film featuring a former military officer illustrates that the soldier is always on duty.
